## Problem Statement
Thousands of satellites orbit Earth, and their positions constantly change due to:
- Atmospheric drag  
- Gravitational forces  
- Orbital maneuvers  

This makes predicting their next position challenging.

Accurate orbit prediction helps prevent:
- Satellite collisions  
- Communication signal loss  
- Mission planning failures  

## Our Solution
We use **Machine Learning (Random Forest)** and **LSTM Deep Learning** to predict the **next orbital position** of a Starlink satellite using its historical orbit data.
